ICTOE AI 2025

About the Conference

The objective of this international conference is to provide a platform for researchers, educators, and industry professionals to explore and share the latest advancements in AI-driven online education. The focus will shift to enhancing global accessibility and equity in education through AI, examining strategies to bridge the digital divide and ensure inclusive access to quality education. The discussion will continue with the integration of AI in pedagogy, exploring how new digital tools can foster personalized learning experiences and improve student engagement. The conference will also address the ethical considerations of AI in education, focusing on privacy, data security, and the impact of AI on the teacher-student dynamic. This conference will bring together experts from leading universities worldwide to share their insights and expertise. It aims to create a collaborative environment where academia, industry, and technology providers can work together to develop innovative AI-powered education solutions. This conference will offer a special venue for students, faculty, and professionals to present their research, engage in discussions on cutting-edge technologies, and contribute to the future of online education.
